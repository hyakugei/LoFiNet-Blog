I'm going to document the development of this game, LoFiNet: Alpha. Quickly at a high level:

  • Online Multiplayer with a hosted Server
  • Persistant Voxel-ish 3D World
  • Top Down Isometric camera
  • 2D sprite based characters
  • Not Minecraft, Stardew Valley or Animal Crossing
    • Although i hope to add some features from all those, eventually.

In order to be a bit different (haha, 18.5k games released last year on steam) from many other online pixel/voxel games, I'm going to be intially concentrating on a skill based, lag-tolerant, press your luck PvP combat system. These will be 1 on 1 duels that players enter into by choice.
